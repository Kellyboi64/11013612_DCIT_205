Our Mission

At the core of our mission lies a commitment to fostering a dynamic learning environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and technical excellence in the field of computer science. We strive to equip our students with comprehensive knowledge and skills...

We aim to empower our graduates to contribute meaningfully to the technology-driven world by emphasizing ethical practices, innovation, and problem-solving abilities.

What We Offer

Study of Computer Science

Our curriculum delves into the study and development of applications, software systems, and ethical means for their reliable functioning in today's Information Technology age.

Programming Skills

Core to our programs is proficiency in programming with modern techniques and tools, applying sound engineering principles to develop and maintain robust software solutions.

Diverse Employment Opportunities

Graduates possess skills for various sectors like software development, cybersecurity, data science, artificial intelligence, and more, contributing specialized expertise to diverse industries.

Platform for Further Study and Research

Our department offers a foundation for continued learning and research in established and emerging technologies, exposing students to advanced methodologies and current trends in computer science.

Degree Programs

We offer comprehensive undergraduate programs: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science - Single-Major (3:2:1:1), Major-Minor (3:2:2:1), and Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, preparing students for diverse career paths.

Specializations and Electives

Students can choose from various specializations such as cybersecurity, data analytics, artificial intelligence, human-computer interaction, and more, enhancing their expertise in specific domains.

Our Vision

We envision becoming a leading hub for computer science education and research, producing graduates not only proficient in technical skills but also adaptable and creative in a globalized world.

We aim to create an inclusive environment that encourages diversity, collaboration, and the use of technology for positive societal impact.

Our Faculty

Our faculty comprises experienced professionals and researchers dedicated to providing high-quality education, mentorship, and guidance to students. They actively engage in cutting-edge research and industry collaborations.

With diverse expertise in various domains of computer science, our faculty members bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom, nurturing the next generation of tech leaders.

Research Initiatives

We are committed to advancing the frontiers of computer science through innovative research initiatives. Our projects span a wide range of areas, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, data science, and more.

Collaborations with industry partners and participation in interdisciplinary research enable us to address real-world challenges and contribute to the evolution of technology.
